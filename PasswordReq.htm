Passwords must meet complexity requirements user passwords meet the following requirements:

  • The password is at least six characters long. Eight characters are required if you'd like to use a Mobile Device with Google Apps for EDU.

  • The password contains characters from at least three of the following five categories:

    • English uppercase characters (A - Z)

    • English lowercase characters (a - z)

    • Base 10 digits (0 - 9)

    • Non-alphanumeric (For example: !, $, ( , ` ) Note: Some characters are incompatible with Banner such as @ % etc.

    • Unicode characters
